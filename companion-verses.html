companion verses ’25

bushido tramadol
glacial doldrums
the eggs in her basket
the face and the swerve
mark the path

I used to the
middle occupied
resurrect + turgid
a tare of wrenches
spinors + myrrh.

the chrome and
oh so magnetised
notion weft of me
a trinket factotum
in freebase array.

the galley requested
to moor by the fishermen
where the ridge gives on
to ocean and to sky.

to the paddock
and the sheepfold
faceted, stained red
for contrast imaging
a view to the growth.

papist changes warp
the transition + basalt
into statues exhibited
before the court +
in the garden.

new findings suggest
two tablespoons and
a bodily unit
when the pulse is strong
and the head is turned.

for it can kill instantly
peregrine and economical
like flowers infrared
cast for a new year
and then tallied up.

sphaerochromatic not
a corrected axis
waning, ebbing mast
flickers channels, noises

cuivers atonicity,
thereabouts parade
industrial flags
churn down there

there dirigible masses
lobster telephones, garçon
stitches Maida Vale tin cans
tin boots & little trowels

pursuant, liable for numbers
hearty great flitters
as if wound, battered
responses code, red & ambergris

sabled flashes, dichromates
little bite of the whip
sediments, sediments & water
passes by the way
DEUS ABSCONDITUS
